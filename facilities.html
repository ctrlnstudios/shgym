Facilities

FREE WEIGHTS

The Sports Hub Gym offers patrons a wide range of free weights suitable for all builds and experience levels, from beginners to professional weightlifters.

With weights of different loads available, all gym goers are able to freely customise their weight training sessions to effectively meet their goals.

KEISER INFINITY

The Keiser Infinity Trainer is one of the core machines under the Infinity series produced by Keiser. It has multiple functionalities which can be used in various ways to get a full-body workout! It gives users the ability to perform a wide variety of exercises that engage various muscle groups, all with just one machine.

The versatility of the Keiser Infinity Trainer makes it suitable for different purposes from strength training to aerobic exercises as well. A unique feature of the Trainer would be the two adjustable arms which give users complete control of the resistance levels that best matches their strength. This way, you can pace yourselves, and customise your workout to best suit your fitness needs.

WATTBIKES

One of the few gyms with Wattbikes, the Sports Hub Gym offers this specially designed exercise bike for cyclists who are looking to perfect their road cycling techniques.

The Wattbike is designed to mimic an authentic road cycling experience through the use of a dual braking system; mainly the air-braking and magnetic braking systems. The air-braking system recreates the feeling of cycling on flat ground, while the magnetic braking system allows riders to experience the motion of climbing.

With this advanced technology, the Wattbike focuses on enhancing users’ experiences by generating accurate and detailed feedback on the cyclists’ technique.

SQUAT RACKS

Squat Racks The Squat Racks available at the Sports Hub Gym are suitable for both beginners and experienced lifters who are training to perfect their squat form.

The squat racks come with adjustable parts, allowing patrons to adjust the bar height according to their shoulder height before proceeding to choose the appropriate weights.

The different loads available assist lifters to progressively improve their form by starting them with weight loads suitable for their current abilities before incrementally adding weights as they progress.

TECHNOGYM MACHINES

Technogym Machines is a leading designer of gym equipment and fitness solutions that provides a diverse range of functionalities that accommodate different cardio, strength and functional training.

With their extensive range of products found in the Sports Hub Gym, patrons are able to customise their workout routines regardless of experience level. All gym goers will be able to achieve their desired results when it comes to meeting their sporting or health goals.

TREADMILLS

Get your daily dose of cardio with our treadmills! Equipped with fully customisable settings, you can adjust to suit the workout you have planned for the day.

With the option to choose your preferred speed and inclination levels, you can decide on the best settings that will help you achieve your fitness objectives. The treadmills also come with features such as heart rate monitors and step counters, allowing you to track your progress easily.
